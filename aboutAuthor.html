About the Author

Barbara Wohlford is a former teacher and historical interpreter. She is the co-author, with Mary Eley, of Places to Take Children in Tidewater Virginia, and Breakfast Inn Style: Historic and Romantic Country Inns of the Southeast and their Signature Recipes. Barbara lives near Washington D.C. in Lansdowne, Virginia.

Other works by Barbara Wohlford

Breakfast Inn Style

By Barbara M. Wohlford and Mary L. Eley

Taste old-fashioned graciousness, southern gentility, and superb cuisine from some of the South's best B&B and country inn kitchens.
Breakfast Inn Style is a remarkable collection of breakfast and brunch dishes from 54 selected historic and out-of-the-way country inns in Alabama, Florida, Georgia, Mississippi, North Carolina, Tenessee, and Virginia. Many of the inns are listed on the National Register of Historic Places, and some others are nestled in picturesque countrysides.

Tidewater Virginia with Children

By Barbara M. Wohlford and Mary L. Eley

Here is the first and only guide for families who want to take in together the many fascinating sights and activities of the beautiful Tidewater Peninsula of Virginia. Separate, easy-to-follow sections describe attractions ranging from the historic triangle of Williamsburg, Jamestown, and Yorktown to the modern-day escitement of Busch Gardens and the Virginia Air & Space Museum.

Tidewater Virginia with Children guves detauked information on local transportation and services, musuems and plantations, amusement and theme parks, harbor cruises and festivals, restaurants and lodging, parks and beaches, and shopping and sports events.

The authors open up the wibders of Tidewater Virginia to families with all kinds of vacation schedules, This is all you'll beed to make the most of one of America's great little treasures.
